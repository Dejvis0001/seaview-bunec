About Us

Experience the serene allure of Seaview Bunec in Piqeras, where every moment harmonizes with the soothing melody of the sea. Delight in culinary wonders at our restaurant, unwind amidst verdant gardens, and sip on refreshing beverages at our bar, all while enjoying complimentary WiFi. Retreat to our cozy guest rooms, featuring private bathrooms and captivating sea views, offering a stylish haven for relaxation and rejuvenation. Begin your day with a sumptuous buffet breakfast, setting the stage for an unforgettable coastal escape at Seaview Bunec.
